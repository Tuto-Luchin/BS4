Flexbox BS 4 Justify and Align on the MAIN and CROSS Axes when ROW

Remember #1 When orientation is row the Main axe is from left to right(X), and Cross axe is at the top to bottom(Y)
Remember #2 When orientation is column the Main axe is at the top to bottom (Y), and Cross axe is from left to right(X)
Use the class justifify-content-(start(default),center, end, between, around)) to justify on Main axe
Use the class align-items-(start, end, center, baseline, or end(default))) to align on Cross axe
